## Freelance Workspace Architecture

Confirmed the Freelance Workspace will remain an internal operations environment rather than a client portal, CRM, or invoicing platform.

Defined the initial freelance workflow:

```text
Lead
→ Discovery
→ Proposal
→ Agreement
→ Project Work
→ Review
→ Completed
```

Established the first workspace structure:

```text
/freelance/projects/
/freelance/client-hub/
/freelance/tasks/
/freelance/templates/
/freelance/pricing/
/freelance/assets/
```

Projects was selected as the first implementation target because it serves as the operational control center for active freelance work.
