### Database Design

Created the `freelance_assets` schema with upload-ready metadata.

Included:

- Nullable `project_id`
- `ON DELETE SET NULL`
- Archive support
- Timestamp tracking
- Indexes
- Upload metadata:
  - `storage_path`
  - `original_filename`
  - `stored_filename`
  - `mime_type`
  - `size_bytes`
