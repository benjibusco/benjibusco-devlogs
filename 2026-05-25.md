## Surface Texture Systems

### Surface Texture Experimentation

Experimented with subtle radial texture overlays to reduce flat digital surfaces and introduce more atmospheric material depth.

Tested multiple approaches including:

- repeating radial gradients
- repeating linear gradients
- scaled texture overlays
- off-card radial origins

Final implementation used:

```css id="w6q2r8"
repeating-radial-gradient(
  circle at 0 0,
  rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.06),
  rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.06) 1px,
  transparent 1px,
  transparent 4px
)
```

Combined with:

```css id="c9v5m3"
background-size: 800px 800px;
background-position: left -200px top -150px;
```

This produced a softer large-scale contour texture that felt more cinematic and integrated into the glass-style dashboard surfaces without visible tiling repetition.
