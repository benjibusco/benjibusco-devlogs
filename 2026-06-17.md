### Card Architecture Exploration

Investigated advanced card rendering techniques including:

- gradient borders
- radial highlights
- layered shadows
- pseudo-element effects
- perimeter glow systems
- reflection treatments

The goal was to create a reusable workspace card system capable of supporting future dashboard modules across the Freelance Workspace.

### Design Findings

Identified several characteristics shared by the preferred reference style:

- brighter upper highlight regions
- stronger lower blue illumination
- darker interior surfaces
- subtle perimeter glow
- variable border intensity
- limited purple usage

Research confirmed that the desired appearance will require a layered visual system rather than a simple border and shadow implementation.
